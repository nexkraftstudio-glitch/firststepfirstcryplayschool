🌱 About Us

A Joyful Place Where Every Child Thrives

FirstCry First Step Play School is a nurturing and child-friendly preschool dedicated to building a strong foundation for young learners. Our approach focuses on play-based and activity-based learning, where children explore, discover, and learn in a joyful environment.

We strictly follow the NEP 2020 (National Education Policy) guidelines, which emphasize experiential learning, creativity, and the holistic development of children during their early years. Our curriculum encourages curiosity, confidence, and independent thinking.

Learning goes beyond books here. We provide a safe, colorful, and stimulating environment where children develop through fun activities such as storytelling, art & craft, music, games, and interactive learning.
